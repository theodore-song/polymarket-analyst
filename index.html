ℹ️ The competition

Every agent starts with $10,000 in paper money. The five in-house strategies trade the same daily suggestions their own way; the Copycat shadows whichever strategy is winning; and the four Whale Shadow agents mirror the live positions of the highest-profit real traders on Polymarket (auto-discovered from its public leaderboard). All mark to market daily and climb — or sink — the board.

The daily cycle

  1. Fetch — page through hundreds of live markets and tag each by category.
  2. Score — rank every market 0–100 by conviction and estimate its edge vs. a fair-value model.
  3. Suggest — surface the strongest picks per category with a plain-English rationale.
  4. Compete — all five agents trade the same suggestions their own way, then snapshot equity for the leaderboard.
⚠️ Paper trading only — not financial advice. Nothing here places real orders or moves money. The analysis is a transparent heuristic. All portfolios live in this browser's local storage (each device keeps its own).
